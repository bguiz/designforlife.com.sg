Night Safari, Singapore

The first project archived here is the world's first night safari. The images are mostly taken with available light to show the real aesthetic impact of the artificial moonlight used. As a consequence the photos suffer from poor focus, motion blur and graininess. They are not presented for their photographic excellence but as just mentioned, to illustrate the lighting effects achieved through techniques (see this paper for details) developed specially for the Night Safari. Note that some photos are in black and white.
